The team

Ripperoni Pizza is a story of five students bringing to life the Italian dream, for their families and their customers. Donald, Alberto, Daniele, Michael and Simone got their start on a long journey in the food business at a young age studing at uniPD. In 2020, the students combined each of their decades of experience and knowledge in the pizza and convenience store industries, founding the company now known as Ripperoni Pizza. They set out to help others achieve a better tomorrow. Today, as at the very beginning of the company, the core mission is to be a blessing.
